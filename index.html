Welcome to Introduction to R Programming

Welcome! In this 7-week course, you will develop foundational R coding skills using RStudio and build AI competencies by exploring intellectual virtues for reproducible, explainable science. You will write R code, reflect on your thinking and understanding, and revise your work for mastery.

This course uses Specifications Grading. 🌱 Core work is required for a C or higher. 🌳 Advanced work is optional and can raise your final grade to a B or A. Work is marked with statuses such as ✅ Complete and 🟡 Not Ready.

Temporary course access: This site provides access to course materials while Canvas access is being restored or clarified. Continue using the module links below for readings, activities, and course resources. Links for grades, discussions, submissions, and progress tracking may be updated as course tools are finalized.
