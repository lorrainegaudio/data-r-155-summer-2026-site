Purpose

This assignment is the final revision step of the 🌳 peer review workflow. First, go back to 🔥 Wildfire Discussion: Peer Review and review the feedback left on your draft. Then revise your work, combine Parts A, B, and C into one final wildfire report, render it to HTML, and submit the updated final version here.

Your revision should show that you read the feedback carefully and made thoughtful decisions about what to improve. Use short Change Memo(s) in your report to explain what you changed, what feedback you responded to, and why you chose that revision path. This final submission should bring together your work from Fire-Assignment-PartA-StudentTemplate.qmd, Fire-Assignment-PartB-StudentTemplate.qmd, and Fire-Assignment-PartC-StudentTemplate.qmd into one complete wildfire report.

At a Glance

  • Time estimate: 30 minutes
  • Due: Monday, Week 8
  • Review feedback in: 🔥 Wildfire Discussion: Peer Review
  • You will submit: one final revised wildfire report as wildfire.html
  • Optional supporting file: wildfire.qmd

Revision

Step C — Final Revision

  1. Return to 🔥 Wildfire Discussion: Peer Review and re-read the feedback you received on your draft.
  2. Make one final wildfire report.
    • Use your work from Fire-Assignment-PartA-StudentTemplate.qmd, Fire-Assignment-PartB-StudentTemplate.qmd, and Fire-Assignment-PartC-StudentTemplate.qmd.
    • Combine those parts into one complete report in a single Quarto file.
    • Make sure the final report includes Parts A, B, and C in one organized document.
  3. Revise your work.
    • Create short Change Memo(s) around your edits.
    • State what you changed.
    • Connect those changes to the feedback you received.
    • Explain why you did not make a suggested change, if you chose not to use part of the feedback.
  4. Include a short learning reflection.
    • What is the most important thing you learned from revising this work?
    • What specific skill, habit, or workflow will you reuse in future coursework?
  5. Render your final report to HTML.
  6. Submit your final revised deliverable here.

What Strong Revision Looks Like

  • the final file is updated, not just re-uploaded unchanged
  • the revision clearly responds to peer feedback
  • the final report combines Parts A, B, and C into one coherent wildfire report
  • the Change Memo(s) help the reader understand what changed and why
  • the learning reflection is short but specific
  • the final report renders cleanly to HTML and is readable from top to bottom

What to Submit

  • Required final file: wildfire.html
  • Optional supporting file: wildfire.qmd

Before you submit: Make sure the HTML file opens, includes Parts A, B, and C in one report, shows your revision work and Change Memo(s), and includes your short learning reflection.

How You Will Be Marked

✅ Complete if:

  • the final deliverable is uploaded, opens, and matches the required artifact
  • the submission is one combined wildfire report that includes Parts A, B, and C
  • Change Memo(s) are included and clearly connect to peer feedback
  • there is clear evidence of revision
  • the submission includes the required learning reflection

🟡 Not Ready if:

  • the final report does not include all three parts
  • Change Memo(s) are missing
  • there is no clear evidence of revision
  • the file will not open
  • the wrong artifact was submitted
  • the required reflection is missing

⭕ No Submission

  • the final file was not submitted

If your submission is marked 🟡 Not Ready, revise to address the missing elements and resubmit before the assignment locks, if allowed.
