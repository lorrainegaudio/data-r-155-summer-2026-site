Specifications Grading

In this course, we use Specifications Grading. Instead of chasing partial credit or worrying about a 79% vs. 81%, your grade is determined by which bundle you complete to a professional standard.

That means each activity is evaluated by whether it meets the requirements. Your job is to complete the required work carefully, revise when needed, and choose whether you want to complete additional Advanced work for a higher final grade.

Quick Legend

Think of this like growing a garden: the 🌱 Core work is the set of “must-grow” seedlings every student needs for a healthy foundation. The 🌳 Advanced work is where you plant extra trees: optional, but they grow your final grade higher.

  • 🌱 Core required work = every item must earn Complete to earn a C or higher.
  • 🌳 Advanced optional work = you choose which items to complete to earn a B or A.

You will see these emojis in assignment titles so you can quickly tell what type of work it is.

What Your Scores Mean

Every activity is graded using simple status labels, not points for partial credit. When you look in your grades, you will see status language like this:

For 🌱 Core work

  • ✅ Complete = meets the specifications.
  • 🟡 Not Ready = revision needed. You can fix and resubmit.
  • ❌ Missing = required work was not submitted.

For 🌳 Advanced work

  • ✅ Complete = counts toward your B or A course grade bundle.
  • 🟡 Not Ready = revision needed. You can fix and resubmit.
  • ⭕ No Submission = you did not choose that option. There is no penalty.

Advanced modules are unlocked when you complete the required Core prerequisites.

The Bundle System

Your final grade is determined by the highest bundle you fully complete.

🥉 C Bundle Requirements

Required: Earn Complete for all 🌱 Core items to earn a C grade or better.

🥈 B Bundle Requirements

Required:

  • Earn Complete for all 🌱 Core assignments, and
  • Earn Complete for at least 12 🌳 Advanced assignments.

🥇 A Bundle Requirements

Required:

  • Earn Complete for all 🌱 Core assignments, and
  • Earn Complete for at least 18 🌳 Advanced assignments.

How to Use the Core and Advanced Bundle Trackers

Core and Advanced trackers help you monitor whether you are meeting the requirements for each grade bundle.

When Canvas is available, use the module completion indicators and grade statuses to check your progress. When Canvas is not available, use this course site to keep working and keep your own notes about which items you have completed, revised, or still need to submit.

What the icons mean in Canvas Modules

  • ✅ Checkmark = requirement met. You earned Complete.
  • 🔵 Blue info icon = submitted but not graded yet. Wait for grading before assuming the requirement is complete.
  • ⭕/➖ Circle or dash icon = requirement not met yet. This often means the item is missing, not submitted, or not ready yet.

Bottom Line

  • To earn a C or better: complete all 🌱 Core work.
  • To earn a B: complete all 🌱 Core work and at least 12 🌳 Advanced assignments.
  • To earn an A: complete all 🌱 Core work and at least 18 🌳 Advanced assignments.

Do the Core work first. Then choose Advanced work strategically based on your time, goals, and confidence.
