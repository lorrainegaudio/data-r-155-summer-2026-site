02.2.05 🤪 Vector MadLib Textbox Submission

🌳 Advanced | 30 min | Module 2

Purpose

This assignment is the submission step for the 🤪 Vector MadLib Advanced Guided Activity.

After you complete the R Notebook template and successfully generate both MadLib stories, you will paste your final story output and a short transparency memo into the Canvas text box. This submission shows that you built the required vectors, ran the story code, checked your work, and can explain your troubleshooting or learning process.

At a Glance

  • Time estimate: less than 30 minutes after completing the guided activity
  • Due: Saturday
  • Complete first: 02.2.04 🤪 Vector MadLib Guided Activity
  • You will submit: Final Story 1 output, final Story 2 output, and a short transparency memo
  • Submission type: Canvas text entry box

Before You Submit

  1. Open your completed Vector MadLib R Notebook in RStudio.
  2. Sweep your Environment using the broom icon.
  3. Run the notebook from top to bottom.
  4. Confirm that both Story 1 and Story 2 print successfully.
  5. Copy the printed story output from your notebook or Console.
  6. Write a short transparency memo explaining one issue you checked, fixed, or verified.

You do not need to submit your R Notebook here. This assignment only collects the final text output and your transparency memo.

What Strong Work Looks Like

  • Both stories clearly come from the R story code, not from a manually written story.
  • The output includes values pulled from the vectors you created.
  • The transparency memo is specific and honest about what you checked, fixed, learned, or verified.
  • If you used outside help, including AI, you name the resource and explain what it helped you understand.

What to Submit

Paste your work directly into the Canvas text entry box. Use the structure below so your submission is easy to read.

Story 1:
[Paste your final Story 1 output here.]

Story 2:
[Paste your final Story 2 output here.]

Transparency Memo:
[Write 3–5 sentences explaining one issue you checked, fixed, or verified. If you used an outside resource, including AI, name the resource and explain what it helped you understand.]

How You Will Be Marked

✅ Complete if:

  • Your submission is pasted into the Canvas text entry box.
  • Story 1 output is included.
  • Story 2 output is included.
  • The stories appear to come from the Vector MadLib R code.
  • Your transparency memo is 3–5 sentences and explains one issue you checked, fixed, learned, or verified.
  • If you used outside help, including AI, your memo names the resource and explains what it helped you understand.

🟡 Not Ready if:

  • Story 1 or Story 2 is missing.
  • The submission appears to be manually written instead of generated from the R story code.
  • The transparency memo is missing or too vague.
  • Outside help was clearly used but not documented.

⭕ No Submission

  • No text entry was submitted.

If your submission is marked 🟡 Not Ready, revise the missing or unclear parts and resubmit before the assignment locks, if resubmission is allowed.

Before You Leave This Page

  • Check that both stories are included.
  • Check that your transparency memo names what you checked, fixed, learned, or verified.
  • Check that any outside help is documented.
  • Make sure your text entry was actually submitted.
