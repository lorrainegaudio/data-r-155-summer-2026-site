01.2.02 🐣 Resilient Beginnings: Getting To Know You

🌳 Advanced | 30 min | Module 1

Purpose

This survey helps you pause at the start of the course and think concretely about who you are as a learner, why you enrolled, and what may affect your progress.

It is connected to the Resilient Beginnings advanced theme and the guided activity Why I’m Learning R, where you begin making realistic decisions about your goals, effort, and course path.

This matters because students usually do better when they name their reasons for learning, notice likely obstacles early, and make a plan for what they will do when things get difficult. Your responses also help me understand how to support you more effectively as the course begins.

What This Survey Asks

  • Share your name and pronouns so I can address you respectfully.
  • Identify the device you will primarily use for coursework to think practically about your setup.
  • Explain why you enrolled in the course and what you hope to gain from learning R.
  • Reflect on the biggest factor most likely to interfere with your success and what you might do to address it.
  • Describe what steps you are most likely to take if you begin to fall behind in the course.

Survey Steps

  • Time estimate: 30 minutes
  • Do: Complete near the start of the course as part of the Resilient Beginnings advanced activities.
  • Before starting: Think honestly about your goals, outside commitments, likely obstacles, and what kind of support or action would actually help you stay on track.
  • Use: Your own experiences, goals, and realistic plans for this semester.
  • Complete: All survey questions, including the questions about your goals, likely barriers, and what you would do if you begin to fall behind.
  • Return to: Submit this survey after you have answered each question thoughtfully.
