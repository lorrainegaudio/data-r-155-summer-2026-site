Purpose

The 🐣 Resilient Beginnings 🌳 Advanced module helps you build a stronger start with R by connecting motivation, planning, peer reflection, and early skill review.

Across these activities, you will reflect on why learning R matters to you, think carefully about your course path, identify likely constraints, share part of your plan with peers, and review beginning R habits such as object names, assignment, scripts, panes, comments, and working directories.

This theme is most useful if you want a thoughtful start to the course: a clearer sense of direction, a more realistic plan, and stronger early habits before later modules ask more from you.

🐣 Resilient Beginnings Learning Goals

By completing this theme, you should be able to make a more intentional start to the course and explain the plan you want to follow.

  • Clarify why you are learning R by connecting the course to your academic goals, work interests, or future plans.
  • Choose a course path intentionally by deciding which bundle best fits your goals, available time, and desired level of challenge right now.
  • Strengthen early learning habits by reviewing foundational course expectations, noticing likely obstacles, and planning realistic first steps.
  • Share and refine your plan with peers by turning private reflection into a discussion that helps you compare choices, ask questions, and think more clearly about next steps.
  • Review early R workflow skills by checking your understanding of RStudio panes, scripts, object names, assignment, comments, and working-directory basics.

Activities in This Theme

This theme includes five connected parts. Start with the guided activity, then continue through the check-in, discussion, reply, and review quiz if this theme feels like a good fit for your start in the course.

  1. 🐣 Resilient Beginnings Guided Activity: Why I’m Learning R

    Plan why you are taking the course and what level of learning fits your goals right now.

    • Time estimate: 45 minutes
    • You will produce: private planning notes and reflection
    • Why it matters: it helps you make your goals explicit before the course pace and workload increase
  2. 🐣 Resilient Beginnings: Getting To Know You

    Complete a short check-in that helps you take stock of your starting point and early course fit.

    • Time estimate: 30 minutes
    • You will produce: survey responses about your learning goals, setup, possible barriers, and support plan
    • Why it matters: it helps you pause, take inventory, and begin the course with a more realistic sense of where you are starting
  3. 🐣 Resilient Beginnings Reflective Discussion: Initial Post

    Share the parts of your plan that you want your peers to know.

    • Time estimate: 30 minutes
    • You will produce: one initial discussion post
    • Why it matters: it turns private planning into a visible starting plan and helps your group understand your goals and choices
  4. 🐣 Resilient Beginnings Reflective Discussion: Reply Post

    Respond to your classmates’ plans and help move the conversation forward.

    • Time estimate: 30 minutes
    • You will produce: two meaningful reply posts
    • Why it matters: it helps you compare approaches, test whether plans are realistic, and support other students as they begin the course
  5. 📖 Review: Module 1.1 Quiz

    Complete a short multiple-choice and fill-in-the-blank review quiz after reviewing Core Module 1.

    • Time estimate: 30 minutes
    • You will produce: quiz completion showing review of early RStudio and script basics
    • Why it matters: it helps you catch small gaps in early R workflow before later modules depend on those skills

Unlock Requirement

This 🌳 Advanced module does not have an unlock requirement. It is available at the start of the course so you can use it to plan your path early.

Guiding Questions and Instructions

This theme works best when you treat it as a chance to think ahead, test your assumptions, and make a realistic start.

Before You Begin

  1. What do you want this course to do for you right now?
  2. Which part of this theme seems most useful for helping you make a realistic start?

While You Work

  1. What are you learning about your goals, your likely constraints, or the amount of effort you can realistically sustain?
  2. If you realize your first plan is not realistic, what will you revise?

After You Finish

  1. What did this theme help you understand more clearly about why you are here and how you want to approach the course?
  2. What is one decision or habit from this theme that you want to carry into the next module?

Before You Leave This Overview

  • Start with 01.2.01 🐣 Resilient Beginnings Guided Activity: Why I’m Learning R.
  • Use your private reflection notes to prepare for the survey and discussion.
  • Remember that the discussion asks you to share only what you are comfortable sharing with peers.
  • Use the review quiz to check early R workflow basics before moving deeper into the course.
